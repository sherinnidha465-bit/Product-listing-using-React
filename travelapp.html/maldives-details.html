Maldives Details

The Maldives is an island nation in the Indian Ocean, known for its crystal-clear waters, pristine beaches, and luxurious overwater bungalows.

It is a popular destination for tourists seeking relaxation, water sports, and romantic getaways.

Trip packages typically bundle essential travel components—flights, accommodation, transfers, and often tours or meals—into a single price for convenience and cost savings. Standard inclusions often feature hotel stays, round-trip airfare, airport transfers, guided sightseeing, and sometimes insurance or porterage

Common Inclusions in Trip Packages:

  • Accommodation in selected hotels or resorts
  • Round-trip airfare to the Maldives
  • Transfers from the airport to the hotel and back
  • Guided sightseeing tours of Maldives' attractions
  • Meals as specified in the package (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
  • Travel insurance coverage (optional)
